# HWI-Safety-Checklist

Standard-compliant safety checklist and hazard analysis for Hot Wire Ignition (HWI) testing based on IEC 60695-2-10/2-11.

## Purpose

This repository provides a comprehensive safety guide and checklist for conducting Hot Wire Ignition (HWI) tests, also known as glow-wire tests, in accordance with international standards IEC 60695-2-10 and IEC 60695-2-11. The materials are intended for training junior engineers and establishing a standard operating procedure (SOP) within electrical testing laboratories.

## Contents

- **Safety-Checklist.md**: A detailed, interactive checklist covering pre-test, during-test, and post-test safety procedures.
- **GitHub Issues**: Hazard analysis with mitigation strategies for three major risks associated with HWI testing.

## Usage

1. Review the Safety-Checklist.md before conducting any HWI test.
2. Ensure all personnel are trained on the checklist and understand each item.
3. Use the hazard analysis issues as a reference for risk assessment and mitigation planning.
4. Adapt the checklist to your specific laboratory setup and local regulations.
